Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 19, 2026

This application is a private, internal data pipeline operated exclusively by XFreight (X-Trux Inc and affiliated entities) for internal business reporting purposes. It is not available to the general public and does not collect data from or about third-party end users.

What this application does

This application connects to QuickBooks Online company files owned and operated by XFreight to retrieve financial data for internal reporting and analysis. Data is written to private cloud storage accessible only to XFreight personnel.

Data collected

The application accesses financial records (profit & loss, balance sheet, cash flow, transactions, and related data) from QuickBooks Online accounts owned by XFreight entities. No personal data belonging to third parties is collected, stored, or shared.

Data storage

All data retrieved is stored in private Microsoft OneDrive storage controlled by XFreight. Data is not shared with any third parties.

OAuth tokens

OAuth access tokens and refresh tokens used to authenticate with QuickBooks Online are stored as encrypted secrets in GitHub Actions and are never exposed or shared outside of XFreight's internal systems.

Contact

For questions about this application, contact the XFreight operations team.
