# Berry Global

Berry Global (formerly Berry Plastics) is a Fortune-500 global packaging manufacturer and an active XFreight customer in 2026. Dan Heeren is the internal point of contact. Detailed accessorial charge rules were shared with dispatch in May 2026.

## Summary

Active trucking relationship via **CH Robinson** (the direct customer in Alvys; Berry Plastics is the underlying shipper). 14 invoices in the May 2026 AR window with amounts ranging $2,125–$4,524/load, all billed as X-TRUX INC. Dan Heeren is the internal owner. Monthly revenue ~$40,000–50,000 estimated from load frequency.

## Key Relationship Structure

**Important:** XFreight's direct customer is **CH Robinson**, not Berry Global. Alvys records these loads as "BERRY PLASTICS / CH ROBINSON" — CH Robinson tenders the loads and pays XFreight, then invoices Berry. XFreight does not have a direct commercial relationship with Berry; losing CH Robinson's berry lanes is the risk, not Berry directly.

## Key Ideas

- **Brokered via CH Robinson** — X-Trux is the asset carrier; CH Robinson is the paying customer; Berry Plastics (now Berry Global) is the shipper.
- **14 loads in May 2026 AR window** — amounts $2,125–$4,524/load; average ~$3,000–3,100/load.
- **Monthly revenue: ~$40,000–50,000** estimated (14+ loads/month × ~$3,000 avg). Confirm from QB P&L.
- **X-TRUX entity** (not X-Linx) — asset hauls, not brokered.
- **Internal owner:** Dan Heeren received and distributed Berry's Truckload Rules and Accessorial Charges to dispatch on May 12, 2026. This governs billing terms.
- **Accessorials matter:** Berry/CH Robinson define their own detention, layover, lumper, and FSC formula — bill exactly to their table or invoices get disputed.
