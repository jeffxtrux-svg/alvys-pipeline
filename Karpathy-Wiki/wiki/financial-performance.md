# Financial Performance

XFreight's financial goals, historical monthly performance, and 2026 YTD snapshot. Source: Jeff's master `Goals and Trends.xlsx` worksheet (last modified 2026-06-04) + QuickBooks pipeline output.

## Summary

Revenue has held roughly steady at ~$5M/year (X-Trux) despite a fleet reduction from 23 trucks (late 2024) to ~15 (mid-2026). Gross margins have improved from 27–28% → 30–31% in 2026. Net margin is ~7.2% on an accrual basis YTD. XFreight is running **~36% below the $649.5K/month revenue goal** primarily due to fleet shrinkage.

## Key Ideas

- **Fleet shrank 35%** (23.4 → ~15 trucks) but revenue held; X-Trux is running fewer but more productive trucks at higher rates.
- **2026 RPM: $2.39** (vs $2.05 in 2025) — best rate in the dataset, reflecting customer-mix discipline.
- **X-Linx revenue has collapsed**: $185K/mo (2024) → $60K/mo (2026). Material gap worth investigating.
- **Empty-mile %** has improved every year: 8.1% (2024) → 7.0% (2025) → 6.7% (2026 YTD) vs. 7.5% goal.

## Recent Capital Events (June 2026)

- **$40K owner capital injection** — Jeff and JB each added $20,000 ($40K total) to the business.
- **Operating loan paid off** — required to onboard [[Factoring]] with Triumph (the factor takes first position on AR). The **trailer was refinanced** to cover the difference and move the deal forward.
- Net effect: working capital freed for the factoring transition; new trailer-refi debt service added in exchange. See [[Factoring]], [[Risk Register]].
