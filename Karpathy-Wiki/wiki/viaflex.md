# Viaflex

Viaflex is a plastic film products manufacturer and one of XFreight's largest revenue customers in 2026. With 17 open X-TRUX invoices plus 2 X-LINX invoices in a 24-day snapshot, and load rates ranging $2,595–$4,770, Viaflex is likely the second-largest X-TRUX revenue source after Billion Automotive.

## Summary

Active, high-frequency account. X-TRUX INC handles the primary hauls (17 invoices, $2,595–$4,770/load); X-LINX INC brokers a smaller portion (~$638/load). Mixed lane profile: Southwest runs (~$2,500–2,900 to Texas) and Northeast/Southeast runs (~$4,100–4,800 to CT, PA, IN). Estimated monthly revenue ~$75,000–85,000 based on the June 2026 open AR snapshot.

## Key Ideas

- **Major revenue account** — estimated $75,000–85,000/month, likely second only to Billion Automotive among X-TRUX customers.
- **Dual entity** — primarily X-TRUX INC (asset hauls); X-LINX INC handles a smaller brokered portion at lower rates (~$638/load vs. $3,878 avg for X-TRUX).
- **Mixed lane profile** — short Southwest hauls (Sioux Falls→Fort Worth TX, ~$2,543) and long Northeast hauls (Sioux Falls→Seymour CT or Harleysville PA, ~$4,111–4,770). Rate split by lane distance.
- **High cadence** — 17 X-TRUX invoices in 24 days = ~21 loads/month from X-TRUX alone.
- **Plastic film / packaging** — dry van freight, consistent with Sioux Falls distribution hub pattern.
