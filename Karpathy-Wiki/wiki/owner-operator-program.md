# Owner-Operator Program

X-Trux's owner-operator program: pay rates, equipment requirements, deductions, and recruiting differentiators. The driver pay leg of the [[Rate-Per-Mile Goal]] cost-out is derived from this program.

## Summary

X-Trux contracts owner-operators (OOs) at **$1.89/mile loaded + empty** (PC Miler practical). No forced dispatch. OTR runs of 2–3 weeks. Samsara ELD + forward-facing camera provided at no cost; no driver-facing camera (deliberate differentiator). Truck must be no older than 5 years.

## Key Ideas

- Same rate for loaded and empty miles is unusual in the industry and a recruiting differentiator.
- No driver-facing camera — explicitly called out in recruiting materials as a differentiator vs. other carriers.
- X-Trux covers PrePass + tolls, Comdata fuel card discounts, quarterly DOT inspections, no trailer rental.
- The OO program has been in place for 24+ years (X-Trux since 1999).
- Hybrid pay model: some OOs are individuals; others are **owner-operator groups** (a truck fleet owner who pays individual drivers — XFreight pays the group/truck owner).

## Pay Rates

| Mile type | Rate (PC Miler Practical) |
|---|---|
| **Loaded miles** | **$1.89/mile** |
| **Empty miles** | **$1.89/mile** |

Rates adjust with fuel prices.

### Additional Pay

| Item | Rate |
|---|---|
| Extra stops | $40/stop (1 pickup + 1 delivery included) |
| Detention | $30/hour after first 2 hours |
| Layover | $200 |

## Settlement Deductions (Standard)

- Fuel + quarterly IFTA fuel tax.
- Insurance (OO uses X-TRUX coverage OR provides own).
- Truck payment if applicable.
- Optional escrow (personal maintenance + tax fund — driver's choice).
- **Licensing: $200/month × 12** covers apportioned plates + 2290 filing.

### Insurance Estimates (if OO provides own)

- Bobtail + physical damage: ~$250–300/week (depends on truck value).
- $125K truck: ~$730/month.
- New truck: ~$1,200/month.

## Truck Requirements

- Must be **no older than 5 years** at contracting.
- Must pass DOT inspection prior to contracting.
- X-TRUX covers **all 4 quarterly inspections** (equivalent to annual DOT standard).

## Operating Territory

- All 48 contiguous states.
- Heavy delivery presence: MA, CT, PA, NC, SC, TN, TX, OH, IL, IA, NE, MN, SD, WA, UT, NV, MO, KS.
- OTR: 2–3 weeks out; 34-hour restart at home when route allows.

## Hybrid Pay Model

Jeff explained to Alvys onboarding (March 2024): *"I have 2 owner operator groups with multiple trucks, we pay the truck, however need a settlement for the driver also. Then we have several owner operators."*

Two structures:
1. **OO Groups** — fleet owner has multiple trucks. XFreight pays the group/truck owner; that owner pays each driver. [[Truk-Way Leasing]] is the payment hub for these groups.
2. **Direct OOs** — XFreight pays the driver directly.

Both flows are represented in Alvys settlements.

## Rate Context for the Cost-Out

Driver pay is the variable leg of [[Rate-Per-Mile Goal]]:
- Effective rate in the cost-out = $1.89/mi base + accessorials, spread across ~7.5% deadhead.
- Trailing 10-day window of settled loads captures the current blended rate.
- The "settled-only" filter matters: freshest loads have $0 driver pay until they settle, which would deflate the per-mile read if included.

## Driver Contract Structure

From an example OO contract (Lacey Campbell file):
- Startup loan: ~$4,880 down + ~$3,000 licensing = ~$7,880 total.
- Repaid through reduced mileage rates over ~32 weeks.
- Signed by Jeff Hannahs (X-Trux) + driver.
