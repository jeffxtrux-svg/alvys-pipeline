# Enertech Global

Enertech Global LLC is an industrial heating / HVAC equipment manufacturer based in Mitchell, South Dakota. XFreight has served as their carrier for 3+ years. A dedicated spotted-trailer account with a consistent outbound lane and occasional longer hauls.

## Summary

Active dedicated account originating in Mitchell SD. Primary lane: Mitchell SD → Greenville IL (730 miles, ~$2,384/load). Secondary longer hauls observed at ~1,460 miles ($4,175–4,300/load). One trailer permanently spotted at Mitchell SD facility. ~3 loads/month observed in June 2026. Dan Heeren dispatches. Entity: X-TRUX INC.

## Key Ideas

- **3+ year account** — established, recurring relationship; Enertech appears in every week of the June 2026 daily uploads.
- **1 dedicated trailer spotted** — trailer 2453248 is permanently stationed at Enertech Global's Mitchell SD facility. Driver hooks, loads, and departs on schedule.
- **Two lane types** — (1) Mitchell SD → Greenville IL short-haul (~730 miles, ~$2,384); (2) longer hauls to unconfirmed destinations (~1,460 miles, ~$4,175–4,300). The longer lane appears less frequent.
- **Mitchell SD origin** — Mitchell is ~70 miles west of Sioux Falls on I-90, a natural truck hub for South Dakota manufacturers.
- **Dan Heeren is the dispatcher** — noted in every daily upload reviewed.

- 2 short Greenville IL hauls (~$2,384 × 2 = $4,768)
