# Key People

Named personnel at XFreight and its key external partners, drawn from email signatures, contracts, and OneDrive documents.

## Summary

XFreight's leadership team consists of four confirmed named employees: **JB Sweere** (President), **Jeff Hannahs** (VP of Business Development), **Audra Newman** (Safety & AP), and **Dan Heeren** (Logistics Manager). Owner-operators are contractors, not employees.

## Key Ideas

- JB and Jeff co-own major decisions (banking, RFPs, capital plans); Jeff owns the tech/sales interface.
- Audra's role spans both safety compliance AND accounts payable — a dual hat common in smaller carriers.
- Dan Heeren manages day-to-day load planning, equipment list, broker communications.
- Driver approvals require **Acrisure/Great West** sign-off before XFreight can hire — Acrisure is effectively a co-decision-maker on hiring.
