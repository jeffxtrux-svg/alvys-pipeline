# Audra Safety Brief — Day 1 Hypothesis

A pre-observation hypothesis of how Audra Newman will use the Safety & Compliance brief on its first day (2026-06-16). Captured by Jeff 2026-06-15 so the envisioned workflow can be compared to observed reality after a few days. **Status: hypothesis — not yet verified.**

## Envisioned Morning Workflow

Jeff's expectation of Audra's flow on first receipt of the redesigned 5am brief:

1. **Settle in.** Arrives, normal morning routine — does not dive into the inbox immediately.
2. **Read the report.** Opens the 5am PDF attachment and reads through it linearly.
3. **Move to Teams.** Goes to the Safety & Compliance Teams channel and responds to the per-owner accountability MessageCards posted after the email (Audra's items first, then Jackson + Dan's shared items).

## Design Intent Behind the Flow

The brief and Teams cards are intentionally coupled:
- **Brief = read-only context.** PDF + email body stub. She does not act inside the email.
- **Teams card = action surface.** Same items, sorted by days-open and severity, so she can reply-and-resolve in the channel the team already uses.
- **3-day carry-forward logic** on accountability items keeps unacknowledged items showing up until she acks them — the brief is the agenda, the Teams thread is the closeout record.

## Open Questions to Ask After Day 3–5

- Does she read the PDF or skim it? Which sections does she read first?
- Does she go to Teams as the next step, or open Samsara / Alvys first to verify before replying?
- Are the Teams cards arriving at the right time (immediately after the 5am email) — or does she want them later, after she has read?
- Anything she is still chasing manually because the brief does not surface it?
- Per-fleet split on tractors (Audra owns X-Trux, shared on Truk-Way with Jackson + Dan) — is the current "shared" label readable, or does she want the action items physically split?
