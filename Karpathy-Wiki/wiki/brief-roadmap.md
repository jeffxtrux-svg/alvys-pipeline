## Page-Placement Rules

Derived from owner conversations (Jeff, 2026-06-15):

- **Facts-shaped pages** → Jeff + executive brief. Jeff counts only what is delivered, billed, and invoiced.
- **Forecast-shaped pages** → JB-shaped by default. The MTD upload is the canonical JB surface. **Exception:** the OTD early-warning page is forecast but Jeff explicitly wants it on the executive brief too — OTD is binary will/won't-deliver (operational), not a financial estimate, so it does not conflict with the facts-vs-forecast split.
- **Safety / compliance pages** → Audra brief. Truk-Way tractor maintenance items are shared with Jackson + Dan; X-Trux fleet tractor + all trailer compliance stay Audra-only or Jackson+Dan-only respectively.
- **Operational / dispatch pages** → Operations brief once it exists. Jackson + Dan own; Jeff sees as cc.

## Why Multiple Briefs

Earlier in June 2026 the executive brief was the only brief — everyone got all 13 pages and most pages were not actionable for most recipients. The split lets each owner receive a brief that is mostly their lane, with cc visibility into others. This is captured in [[Employee Responsibilities]].

## Pages That May Eventually Migrate Executive → Operations Brief

When the Operations brief is built, candidates to move (or duplicate):

- Driver mileage by settlement week (page 7 today on executive)
- Fleet operations MPG/speeders (page 8 today)
- Fleet idle (page 9 today)
- OTD early-warning page (new — on executive brief first per Jeff, then also on Operations brief)

Open question: do operational pages stay on the executive brief also, or move entirely? Jeff has said he wants at least OTD on both. Default for others until asked: **duplicate** (both briefs render the same page) rather than move.
