# SBA 504 Financing

A combined real-estate-plus-business-purchase transaction of ~$3M being evaluated by XFreight as of February 2026. Structured as an SBA 504 loan. Status: reviewing cash flows; awaiting term sheet from the 504 partner as of the email thread (Feb 2026).

## Summary

XFreight is evaluating an SBA 504-structured transaction to purchase "all current tractors, trailers, property, and the business purchase" — likely acquiring the building they operate from and/or consolidating ownership of Truk-Way Leasing assets. The deal requires ~$230K cash injection and is expected to bring the **N&J Properties** and **N&J Trailers** QuickBooks entities online. Status: cash flow review in progress as of Feb 2026; no closure confirmation found as of seed date.

## Key Ideas

- ~$3M total project; ~$230K cash down payment required.
- Monthly debt service after closing estimated $20–25K/month.
- If the lease-to-own shift happens, the **Lease Payments** line in the [[Cost Per Mile]] changes to interest + depreciation.
- N&J Properties LLC + N&J Trailers (currently dormant QB entities) are expected to go live after closing.
- The $43,739 [[Acrisure Dispute]] credit (if applied before closing) directly improves the cash position.

## Deal Structure (Typical SBA 504)

| Tranche | % | Estimated $ |
|---|---|---|
| First mortgage (First Dakota Bank) | ~50% | ~$1.5M |
| SBA 504 / CDC loan | ~40% | ~$1.2M |
| Borrower equity | ~10% | ~$300K (cash ~$230K + other ~$70K) |

## What's Being Purchased

Jeff's email: *"all current tractors, trailers, property, and the business purchase."*

Possible interpretations:
1. Acquiring the building XFreight currently rents (→ N&J Properties LLC).
2. Acquiring Truk-Way Leasing's trailer/equipment inventory (→ N&J Trailers).

The N&J entities in QuickBooks (currently dormant — no refresh tokens) point toward real-estate (N&J Properties) and additional trailer inventory (N&J Trailers) being the post-closing structure.
