## What it is

A daily executive brief, sent every morning at **5:00am Central** year-round by `.github/workflows/scorecard_email.yml`. 13 pages, rendered to PDF by WeasyPrint, scoped to **X-Trux + X-Linx** (JW Logistics excluded throughout).

Recipients (current as of 2026-06-05):
- jeff@xfreight.net
- jb@xfreight.net

## How it's built

- **Workflow:** `.github/workflows/scorecard_email.yml`. Same dual-cron + CT-hour-gate pattern as the rest of the pipeline. Primary cron is `0 10 UTC` (5am CDT) plus four backup retries through ~6am.
- **Reads, doesn't pull.** The email job is **read-only** — it pulls nothing from the source APIs, only reads the staged OneDrive workbooks the 4am pulls just wrote (`Alvys Master 2026.xlsx`, `Samsara_Master.xlsx`, the QB `.xlsx` files, `SambaSafety_Master.xlsx`).
- **Same-day idempotency marker** in OneDrive prevents double-emailing across the 5 backup cron slots.
- **Manual workflow_dispatch bypasses the same-day marker** so on-demand resends actually send.

## Page structure (13 pages, grouped into 4 sections)

### 1. Overview (Page 1)

The executive summary. Includes:
- **Bottom Line** — narrative paragraph generated by `scorecard_insights.bottom_line()`. Names drivers triggering speed-over-limit escalations (STOP / sit-down tiers, excluding any showing improvement). Names trailers overdue on the 120-day company DOT policy. Calls out AR / margin / RPM gaps.
- **Entity P&L** — X-Trux + X-Linx revenue, margin, miles, loads.
- **AR / AP trend tiles** — 6-month balance trend mini-charts.
- **AR tiles** — 5 buckets (Current / 1–30 / 31–60 / 61–90 / 91+) wrapped in a nested 5-col table because the outer brief container is fixed at 4 columns.
- **QB-vs-Alvys AR reconciliation** — variance + 61+ spot-check.
- **Safety tiles** — 24h / 7d / MTD safety event + HOS violation + DVIR defect counts.
- **6-month safety trend** — monthly bar chart of safety events.
- **X-Trux rate-per-mile goal** — the cost-out. Live driver-pay/mi + pinned office overhead/mi ÷ target operating ratio. Four tiles + a 6-month trend chart.
- **Safety event detail tables** (last 7 days) — HOS violations, safety events (with driver Ack column), DVIR defects, Coaching needs assigned (Monitor rolls off at 7d, Assign coaching keeps until acked + 3d).

### 2. SAFETY (Pages 2–6)

- **Page 2: Driver compliance** — SambaSafety MVR + license status, DOT medical-card expirations from Alvys.
- **Page 3: Safety & compliance detail** — last 24h events / HOS violations / DVIR defects / coaching. Driver Ack column on safety events.
- **Page 4: Per-driver Samsara safety scores** — split off page 3. Speed-Over-Limit % table with 6mo / 3mo / MTD windows + per-driver comment ("STOP this driver now" / "Need to sit down" / "Driver needs a conversation" / "Watch this driver" + trend phrases).
- **Page 5: Equipment compliance — tractor inspections.**
- **Page 6: Equipment compliance — trailer inspections.**

### 3. OPERATIONAL (Pages 7–9)

- **Page 7: Driver mileage by settlement week.** Wed 3pm CT → Wed 2:59pm CT cycle. 5 weeks shown. Below-target tile uses `DRIVER_TARGET_MILES = 2750` mi/wk.
- **Page 8: Fleet operations — MPG.** Best/worst 5 trucks MTD. Fleet miles · MTD tile (sourced from Samsara Trips with the `endMs` MTD filter properly applied + `_is_excluded_truck` exclusion).
- **Page 9: Fleet idle.** All trucks ranked by avg idle/wk over 5 settlement weeks. Idle %, idle gallons est. (`idle_hours × 0.8 gph` fallback), MPG per truck.

### 4. CSA SCORECARD (Page 10)

- **Page 10: FMCSA carrier scorecard** for X-Trux (DOT #841776 / MC #375851). BASIC percentile ranks from the SambaSafety CSA2010 Preview Scorecard CSV. INTERVENTION LIKELY flag at FMCSA thresholds (65th for Unsafe Driving + Crash Indicator, 80th for all others). Fails soft to a "data unavailable" page if the CSV is absent from OneDrive.

### 5. ACCOUNTING (Pages 11–13)

- **Page 11: AR overdue + Alvys accounting.** Top section is the QB AR overdue list (31+). Lower section is the un-invoiced gap (delivered loads not yet billed) + the 90+ collections list.
- **Page 12: QB-vs-Alvys reconciliation by customer.** Per-customer variance; rows sum to the page-1 variance.
- **Page 13: Bill-by-bill matching.** Per-invoice match between Alvys invoice # / Load # and QB `Num`, with `_norm_inv` stripping the leading "T" prefix (QB convention).

## Key constants the brief reads

In `src/scorecard_email.py`:

- `COACH_EVENT_THRESHOLD = 2` — drivers with ≥ this many safety events in window need coaching.
- `DRIVER_TARGET_MILES = 2750` — weekly miles target for the below-target tile.
- `VIOLATION_WINDOW_DAYS = 90` — MVR violation lookback (page 2 + page-1 tile).
- `LICENSE_EXPIRY_WARN_DAYS = 60` — license expiring-soon tile window.
- `SAMBA_HIGH_RISK_SCORE = 16` — fallback threshold when no category column.
- `RPM_GOAL_TARGET_OR = 0.95` — operating ratio for the goal calc (5% net margin).
- `RPM_GOAL_OVERHEAD_PIN = 0.98` — hand-set overhead $/mi while the live calc is being validated.
- `XLINX_MARGIN_GOAL = 17.5%` — brokerage margin target.
- `MEDICAL_EXPIRY_WARN_DAYS = 30` and `DRIVER_EXPIRY_CRITICAL_DAYS = 14` — DOT medical card windows.
