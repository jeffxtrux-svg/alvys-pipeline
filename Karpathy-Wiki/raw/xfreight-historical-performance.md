## Key trend observations

### Fleet shrinkage
- **Aug 2024 → Apr 2026:** 24 trucks → 20 trucks (Apr 2026 rebounded from a low of 18 in Feb/Mar)
- The Nov 2025 dip to 18 trucks coincides with revenue dropping to $364K (lowest in the dataset)

### Rate recovery in 2026
- 2025 RPM held around $2.10
- 2026 RPM jumped to $2.65 in April — **highest in two years**. Market improving or rate-discipline working.

### Margin expansion
- 2024-2025 margins clustered around 27-28%
- 2026 YTD margins are **30-31%** — material improvement
- Driver pay leg flat at ~$1.49 to $1.89/mi; gains coming from rate discipline + cost containment

### Empty miles
- Goal: 7.50%
- 2024 avg: ~8.1% (645 empty mi/truck ÷ 7948 total)
- 2025 avg: 7.0% (746 ÷ 10689)
- 2026 YTD: 6.7% (650 ÷ 9692)

Empty-mile percentage has improved year-over-year. **2026 is currently beating the goal.**
