## Jeff's envisioned workflow (captured 2026-06-15, before Day 1)

1. **Settle in.** Audra arrives, coffee, normal morning routine — does
   *not* dive into the inbox immediately.
2. **Read the report.** Opens the 5am brief (PDF attachment), reads
   through it linearly.
3. **Move to Teams.** Goes to the Safety & Compliance Teams channel and
   responds to the per-owner accountability MessageCards the workflow
   posts after the email (Audra's items first, then Jackson + Dan's).
   Reply on the card = action taken / acknowledgement.

## Why this matters

The brief design and the Teams accountability webhook are intentionally
coupled to this flow:

- **Brief = read-only context** (PDF + email body stub). She doesn't act
  inside the email.
- **Teams card = action surface** with the same items, sorted by
  days-open and severity, so she can reply-and-resolve in the channel
  the team already lives in.
- The 3-day carry-forward logic on accountability items exists so
  carried items keep showing up until she acks them — the brief is the
  agenda, the Teams thread is the closeout record.
