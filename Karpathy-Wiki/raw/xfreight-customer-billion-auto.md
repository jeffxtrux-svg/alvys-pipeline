## Lanes

### Lane 1: Sioux Falls SD ↔ Rapid City SD (daily)

- Daily service Sunday – Friday
- **Spotted trailer** on-site at Billion Auto Parts in Sioux Falls before 4:00 PM daily
- Pickup trailer in Sioux Falls between **1:00–1:30 AM** on specified days
- Delivers to Billion Kia in Rapid City at **5:00 AM**
- **Rates:** $900 SF→RC + $300 RC→SF = **$1,200 round trip**

### Lane 2: Sioux Falls SD → Worthington MN → Mason City IA → Worthington MN → Sioux Falls SD

- Daily service Sunday – Friday
- Spotted trailer on-site at Billion Auto Parts in Sioux Falls before 12:00 PM
- Pickup trailer in Sioux Falls between **4:30–5:00 PM**
- Delivers to Worthington MN 6:00–6:30 PM
- Delivers to Mason City IA 8:45–9:15 PM
- Unassisted pickup in Worthington MN 1:00–1:30 PM
- Drop trailer in Sioux Falls before 4:00 AM
- **Rate:** $950 per round trip daily, all inclusive

## Combined-lane provision

The agreement keeps the two lanes as a **combined rate agreement** — if one lane is removed, either party can exit. So Billion can't cherry-pick the better-rate lane and drop the worse one without ending the whole deal.

## Adjustment clause

If FSC (fuel surcharge) increases or decreases by a substantial amount, both parties can review/renegotiate rates.

## Termination

Either party can withdraw due to service failures or unforeseen circumstances. **Not a freight volume guarantee** — this is a rate agreement only.
