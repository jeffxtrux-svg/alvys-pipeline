## Why this matters

This worksheet is **the hand-maintained source for what XFreight *should* be doing** vs the brief's live measurement of what it *is* doing. Reconciling the two is how Jeff knows whether the business is on plan:

- Page-1 entity P&L tiles compare actual revenue/margin to these targets
- The X-Trux RPM Goal cost-out (live) computes the *break-even* rate; this worksheet sets the *profitable* target
- Empty-mile actuals (from Alvys) compare to the 7.50% goal
- Driver per-truck mileage actuals compare to the 2,800 mi/wk goal (which is $336K in trucks-worth of utilization at the trucking revenue target)
