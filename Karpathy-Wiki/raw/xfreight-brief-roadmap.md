## Page-placement rules emerging from owner conversations

- **Forecast-shaped pages** = JB-shaped by default. Exception: the OTD
  early-warning page is forecast but Jeff explicitly wants it on the
  executive brief too — because OTD is binary will/won't-deliver
  (operational), not a financial estimate, so it doesn't conflict with
  the facts-vs-forecast split.
- **Facts-shaped pages** = Jeff + executive brief.
- **Safety / compliance** = Audra brief. Tractor-maintenance items on
  Truk-Way fleet are shared with Jackson + Dan (per responsibility
  map); X-Trux fleet tractor + all trailer compliance stay Audra-only
  or Jackson+Dan-only respectively.
- **Operational / dispatch** = Operations brief once it exists.
  Jackson + Dan own; Jeff sees as cc.

## Why split into multiple briefs

Earlier 2026-06 the executive brief was the only brief — everyone got
all 13 pages every morning, and most pages weren't actionable for
most recipients. The split lets each owner read a brief that's mostly
their lane, with cc visibility into others. Captured in the
employee-responsibilities map: brief ownership follows function
ownership.
