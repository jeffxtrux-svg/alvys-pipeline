# AGCO RFP outcome — NOT AWARDED (seeded 2026-06-05 from Outlook)

> Source: Jeff Hannahs ↔ Erik Arana (AGCO Logistics Mgr) email thread,
> Jan 20, 2026.

## Result

**XFreight was NOT awarded any lanes in the 2026 AGCO Truckload RFP.**

This is the final outcome of the bid documented in `xfreight-customer-agco-rfp.md`. Despite several lanes "showing positive signs initially," the awards published in January 2026 did not include XFreight.

## Direct quote from Jeff

> *"We had several lanes that were showing positive signs initially, but as we know, the awards have been published yet... we were not awarded any lanes. Hopefully, we will have another opportunity in the future."*
> — Jeff Hannahs to Erik Arana, AGCO Corporation, January 20, 2026

## Implications

- **Revenue impact:** The AGCO opportunity was up to $5-10M+/year in potential. None of it landed.
- **Capacity freed:** XFreight didn't have to onboard for AGCO iTMS, didn't have to add reefer capacity for Hazmat/Temp Control lanes, didn't have to factor against AGCO's 60-day payment terms.
- **Strategic note:** Bid pricing for an asset-based carrier (X-Trux) vs. asset-light brokerage competitors was likely the gap. AGCO's stated criteria included "best pricing for that lane" and "low-cost carrier" tendering.

## Relationship

The door is open for future RFPs:
- Erik Arana (Manager, Logistics NA) replied politely; XFreight thanked him for the opportunity
- Both sides expressed interest in future business
- The 2026 RFP rates are honored until Jan 31, 2027 — next bid cycle likely **late 2026 / early 2027** for 2027 rates
